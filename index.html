Who is Roslyn Smith?

Roslyn Smith is a survivor of the criminal injustice system, who served 39 years in captivity.

She currently works for Vday as the Beyond Incarceration Program Manager.

Visit Vday.org to learn about her work

Roslyn was released on October 15, 2018. She enjoys cooking, spending time with her daughter and friends and playing with her cat Phoenix. She is passionate about the work she is doing to end mass incarceration.
